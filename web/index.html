Things That Exist Online

  • Grant Sanderson's 3B1B

    I absolutely love what Grant Sanderson is doing with mathematics education (and technical education more broadly) with videos on motivated intuitions behind otherwise potentially quite hard to grasp topics. The Manim python library along with the community around it is also yet another sign that what he is doing is not just amazing in its own right but has real potential to be the basis for lots of new amazing educational content on the web!

    Also he got me out of calculus one — just in case I needed any more reasons to be in awe of the project.

  • Zoe Bee

    Another person who I believe is doing some incredible things on the internet. I love all of her videos so much, but her passion for education is especially heartwarming and inspiring to me. I sincerely believe that some of the videos on her channel have made me a better person after some reflection and that is always a good thing.

    Also her cat is so cute and shows up in many videos which... it just adds that much more. From her current page description: The cat is the brains of the operation.
