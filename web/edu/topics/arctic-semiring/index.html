A Brief Disambiguation

This page is primarily referencing the max-plus semiring. This may appear in some literature as the tropical semiring or simply the max semiring. Whenever a different semiring is mentioned I will use the full name, for example the min-plus semiring, otherwise assume that equations are within the max-plus (arctic) semiring.
