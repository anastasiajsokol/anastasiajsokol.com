Welcome!

This is the page (or collection of pages) I am most excited about within the entire website! There are currently only a few published topics (all listed above) but hopefully more will be coming soon. The short-term trajectory of this site is aimed at covering small, primarily stand-alone, topics that can help build understanding if they are encountered somewhere else. Some will be general overviews of a topic, useful if that aligns with a specfic goal. Others are planned to be iteratively deeper looks into a topic, useful if the goal is to build more robust general knowledge. The long-term vision is to eventually cover enough topics to start building meaningful interrelated context around the topics themselves. Some will inevitably remain just tiny peeks into something specific, but others could be organized into groups, guides, and finally courses.

Please feel free to propose topics you want to see here, give recommendations on how to further evolve the site, or tell me about all the things I got wrong via my contact information.
