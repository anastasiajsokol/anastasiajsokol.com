Why Have a Javascript CDN?

I dislike depending on other websites to provide javascript content that is essential to the operation of a page itself. The difference between the CDN section of the archive and the core archive itself is that the CDN section is hosting primarily content that is not in any way owned by me (Anastasia).

Also the naming scheme is different to allow for both the origin and the content of the files to be more clear from the URL itself. This makes it easier to link to within page content in a way that will not be aggravating to me.
